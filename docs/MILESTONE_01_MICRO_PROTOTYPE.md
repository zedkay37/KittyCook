# Milestone 01: Micro Prototype Fun

Goal: prove that KittyCook is funny before producing content.

Duration target: 1 to 2 weeks.

## Product Goal

Two players should complete a short greybox kitchen service, understand their
mistakes, laugh at least once, and want to replay.

## Required Features

- Browser build.
- Isometric 3D camera.
- Two local players.
- Keyboard input.
- Basic gamepad input.
- Pickup and drop.
- Cutting station.
- Cooking station with burned state.
- Service station.
- One active customer order.
- Basic score.
- Round timer.
- One readable accident.
- One simple feline mechanic.

## Acceptance Criteria

- Two players can finish a 3-minute round.
- A new player understands the objective in less than 2 minutes.
- At least 3 out of 5 test groups laugh spontaneously.
- At least 60 percent of groups ask to replay.
- No player confuses cutting, cooking, and serving stations.
- Frame rate stays above 50 FPS on a mid-range laptop.

## Explicit Exclusions

- Online co-op.
- Phone controllers.
- Voice commands.
- Webcam interactions.
- Versus mode.
- Campaign map.
- Final art pass.
- More than two levels.

## Playtest Notes To Record

- Time to first laugh.
- Number of spontaneous communications per minute.
- Number of "what do I do?" moments.
- Errors understood versus errors not understood.
- Replay request after the first round.
- Any input or camera complaint.

## Go / No-Go

Go to the Playable Prototype only if the greybox loop creates laughter and
replay desire.

If not, change movement, interaction, hazard timing, or level layout before
adding content.
